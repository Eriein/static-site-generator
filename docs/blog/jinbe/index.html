Who is Jinbe?

Jinbe is the helmsman of the Straw Hat Pirates, a whale shark Fish-Man and a master of Fish-Man Karate. He once held a seat among the Seven Warlords of the Sea but gave the title up rather than turn his weapons on Whitebeard's allies. By the time he joins the crew he's already been a Sun Pirates captain, an Impel Down prisoner, and the man who kept Luffy alive when he had nothing left after Marineford.

He asked to join the Straw Hats at Whole Cake Island, then made Luffy wait until he'd settled his old debts before he came aboard.

Fun Facts

  • He's a whale shark Fish-Man whose Fish-Man Karate can strike through water itself.
  • He shares Luffy's blood type and gave him a life-saving transfusion after Marineford, defying the taboo against Fish-Men donating blood to humans.
  • He gave up his Warlord title rather than fight against Whitebeard and his allies.
  • He captained the Sun Pirates after their founder, Fisher Tiger.
  • He can manipulate the surrounding seawater and hurl it like a weapon.

Best Moments

  • Marineford — shielding an unconscious Luffy through the climax of the war and carrying him out alive, without which there's no second half of the story.
  • "Fill the hole in Luffy's heart" — after Ace's death, calmly listing everything Luffy still has left to live for.
  • Leaving Big Mom — sitting across from an Emperor and telling her to her face that he's done, negotiating every blow she throws.
  • Jinbe vs. Who's-Who — his best fight, Fish-Man Karate at full force against a former CP9 agent, with a history lesson delivered mid-battle.

Final Thoughts

Jinbe waited longer than anyone to board that ship, making sure his crew was safe and his debts were paid before he'd let himself join. That patience is the whole of who he is — and when he finally takes the helm of the Sunny, you know the crew is in steady hands.
