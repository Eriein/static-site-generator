Who is Usopp?

Usopp is the sniper of the Straw Hat Pirates, the son of one of Shanks' crew, and a compulsive liar who once invented an entire alter ego rather than face a battle as himself. He's also one of the most human characters in the series — a normal person who somehow ended up surrounded by monsters.

He grew up without his father in a quiet village, telling tall tales to cheer up a sick girl named Kaya. He'd run through town every morning shouting that pirates were coming. When real pirates finally showed up, he ran straight at them.

Fun Facts

  • His father, Yasopp, is the sniper of Shanks' Red Hair Pirates.
  • His design and long nose are a deliberate nod to Pinocchio, the most famous liar in fiction.
  • He fights with the Kabuto slingshot and Pop Greens — plant-based ammo he learned to grow on the Boin Archipelago.
  • His alter ego "Sogeking" hides behind a mask, a cape, and his own theme song.
  • His birthday is April 1 — April Fools' Day.

Best Moments

  • Usopp vs. Luffy — he challenges his own captain over the fate of the Going Merry, and loses, in the most emotionally tangled scene of the Water 7 arc.
  • "I am Sogeking!" — a ridiculous disguise that somehow becomes one of the coolest reveals in the series.
  • Burning the flag at Enies Lobby — declaring war on the World Government with a single shot, hands shaking the whole time.
  • God Usopp vs. Sugar — knocking out the linchpin of Doflamingo's control over Dressrosa by pulling the most horrifying face imaginable.

Final Thoughts

Usopp is the audience's stand-in: openly scared, fully aware he's outmatched, and stubborn enough to fight anyway. His climb from coward to "God Usopp" is the most relatable arc in the series precisely because the fear never fully goes away.
