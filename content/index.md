## Reasons I like One Piece

- You can spend years watching or reading about it and still find new things you didn't know about it
- It can be enjoyed by children and adults
- The world-building is unmatched in all of manga. Just watch the [breathtaking world of One Piece](https://youtu.be/AcSVkCNDLZg?si=NYo_NgXWz2SSs_VC)
- Oda adds so many fun things if you really look at it in detail.
