## Who is Franky?

Franky is the shipwright of the Straw Hat Pirates and a self-built cyborg who rebuilt his own body after a near-fatal accident, running himself on cola and salvaged parts. He's loud, dramatic, fond of striking poses, and he built the Thousand Sunny — the ship that's meant to carry the crew to the end of the Grand Line.

He grew up in Water 7 as the apprentice of Tom, the master shipwright who built Gold Roger's ship, the Oro Jackson. Ship-building has been his whole life ever since.

## Fun Facts

- His real name is Cutty Flam; "Franky" is the identity he took on in Water 7.
- He turned himself into a cyborg after being run over by a sea train.
- He runs on cola and weakens badly when his supply runs dry.
- He built the Thousand Sunny from Adam Wood, the same rare lumber used for Roger's ship.
- His mentor Tom built the Oro Jackson, the ship that carried the future Pirate King.

## Best Moments

- **Coup de Burst** — the Sunny's signature launch, with Franky powering it and roaring into the sky.
- **Franky vs. Señor Pink** — two hardboiled men with brutal pasts trading punches and tears, one of the most unexpectedly moving fights in Dressrosa.
- **General Franky** — Builds a robot to fight. Who doesn't like robots!

## Final Thoughts

Franky is the crew's builder — of ships, of morale, of ridiculous situations. The Thousand Sunny is practically a character of her own, and she exists because Franky poured everything he had into her.
