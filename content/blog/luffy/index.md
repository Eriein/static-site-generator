## Who is Luffy?

Monkey D. Luffy is the captain of the Straw Hat Pirates and the center of everything One Piece is about. As a kid he ate a Devil Fruit that turned his body to rubber — long believed to be the Gum-Gum Fruit, but revealed during the Wano arc to actually be the Hito Hito no Mi, Model: Nika, a Mythical Zoan. His goal is simple: find the One Piece and become King of the Pirates.

What makes him work as a protagonist isn't the power. It's that he gathers people by example rather than by force, and almost everyone he meets ends up wanting to fight for their own dream because of him.

## Fun Facts

- His favorite food is meat, and he'll eat almost anything that isn't nailed down.
- The straw hat was lent to him by Shanks and is a Roger-era heirloom he's promised to return one day.
- His Devil Fruit was disguised as a common Paramecia for decades — it's actually a Mythical Zoan tied to the legend of the "Sun God" Nika.
- After Wano, his bounty climbed to 3,000,000,000 berries and he was named one of the Four Emperors.
- He's been voiced by Mayumi Tanaka in the anime since it began in 1999.

## Best Moments

- **Marineford** — fighting through the entire war alongside the Impel Down escapees to reach Ace, and the breakdown that follows when he can't save him.
- **Gear 5 vs. Kaido** — the most absurd and joyful fight in the series, where his power and his personality finally become the same thing.
- **The "I can't do anything alone" speech** — admitting he can't fight with a sword, navigate, cook, or even lie, and declaring he'll be Pirate King anyway because that's exactly why he needs a crew.
- **The vow to Shanks** — a kid making a promise to a legend, and the moment the whole series grows out of.

## Final Thoughts

Luffy isn't the smartest or the most calculating character in One Piece, and he wins mostly by refusing to lose. Carried with total sincerity across more than a thousand chapters, that's turned out to be more than enough.
