## Who is Chopper?

Tony Tony Chopper is the doctor of the Straw Hat Pirates and a reindeer who ate the Human-Human Fruit, giving him human intelligence, speech, and the ability to shift between several forms. Shunned by his herd for his blue nose and abandoned by people for being a talking animal, he was taken in by the quack doctor Hiruluk and later trained by Dr. Kureha.

He joined the crew carrying one goal above all others: to become a doctor who can cure any disease.

## Fun Facts

- He ate the Hito Hito no Mi (Human-Human Fruit), a Zoan that gave a reindeer a human mind.
- The Rumble Ball is his own invention, letting him unlock transformations beyond his natural ones.
- His first bounty was a mere 100 berries because the Marines assumed he was the crew's pet.
- He's a reindeer but gets mistaken for a tanuki constantly, which genuinely annoys him.
- His mentor Hiruluk wasn't a real doctor at all — Kureha trained him into a doctor.

## Best Moments

- **"That man was my father!"** — Hiruluk's death and the funeral that follows, one of the heaviest stretches in the whole series.
- **Mastering Monster Point** — returning after the timeskip finally able to control the transformation that once nearly killed him.
- **Chopper vs. Kumadori** — overdosing on Rumble Balls and losing control as a rampaging Monster Point to protect the crew.
- **Onigashima** — brewing an antidote to Queen's Ice Oni plague while battling the All-Star himself in Monster Point.

## Final Thoughts

Chopper proves the smallest crewmate can carry the most emotional weight. He's there for every injury and every recovery, the reason the crew survives long enough to keep going, and he's well on his way to the dream that started it all.
