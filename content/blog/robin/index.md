## Who is Robin?

Nico Robin is the archaeologist of the Straw Hat Pirates and the only living person able to read the Poneglyphs, the ancient stones that record the world's true history. The World Government branded her a criminal as a child for that ability, wiped out her home island of Ohara to bury what she might uncover, and hunted her across the seas before she was old enough to understand why.

She spent two decades surviving by joining and betraying one group after another, trusting no one, until she met Luffy.

## Fun Facts

- She's the only person alive who can read the Poneglyphs — the entire history of the world runs through her.
- She received the first bounty ever placed on a child: 79,000,000 berries at the age of eight.
- Her Devil Fruit, the Hana Hana no Mi (Flower-Flower Fruit), lets her sprout copies of her limbs on any surface.
- She survived the destruction of Ohara because the giant Jaguar D. Saul smuggled her out.
- Saul also taught her to laugh — the "dereshishishi".

## Best Moments

- **"I want to live!"** — at Enies Lobby, Robin finally admits the thing she's never let herself believe, and the crew tears down a fortress to answer her.
- **The Ohara flashback** — her childhood and the scholars who died protecting their library and her.
- **Robin vs. Black Maria** — her Wano fight, where she cuts loose for the first time in years with a giant "Demonio Fleur."
- **Hunting the true history** — every Poneglyph she reads brings her closer to the dream the whole world tried to kill her for.

## Final Thoughts

Robin earned her place on the crew the hard way, carrying both the weight of a lost civilization's history and twenty years of being told she had no right to exist. She's also dry, funny, and a little morbid — and there's no one else in the series like her.
