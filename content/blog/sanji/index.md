## Who is Sanji?

Sanji is the cook of the Straw Hat Pirates and a master of the Black Leg Style, a fighting style built entirely around kicks so that his hands stay safe for cooking. He was born into the Vinsmoke family — royalty and assassins of the Germa 66 — but he walked away from all of it to live as a chef.

He survived being stranded on a barren rock as a boy thanks to Zeff, the pirate-turned-cook who kept them both alive, and he's chased the same dream ever since: the All Blue, a legendary sea said to hold fish from every ocean in the world.

## Fun Facts

- He fights only with his legs to keep his hands uninjured for the kitchen.
- When he and Zeff were stranded, Zeff secretly went without food and lost a leg to keep Sanji alive — Sanji didn't learn the truth until much later.
- He was born a Vinsmoke, part of the Germa 66 royal family, but rejected the name.
- His dream is to find the All Blue, where fish from every sea are said to gather.
- He flatly refuses to fight women, a principle the story repeatedly puts him in danger over.

## Best Moments

- **Sanji vs. Jabra** — the debut of Diable Jambe, where he reveals he can set his own leg on fire through sheer friction.
- **Whole Cake Island** — his most painful arc, dragged back into the family he escaped and forced to choose between his blood and his crew.
- **Stranded with Zeff** — the flashback that explains everything about him, where surviving starvation becomes the root of his belief that no one should ever go hungry.
- **Ifrit Jambe vs. King** — the moment it's clear he's grown into something far beyond just the crew's cook.

## Final Thoughts

Sanji is the crew's heart in a quieter way than Luffy. Luffy gives people a reason to fight; Sanji makes sure they're fed and standing well enough to do it, and he never asks for credit.
