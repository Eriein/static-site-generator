## Who is Nami?

Nami is the navigator of the Straw Hat Pirates and the reason the crew can actually cross the Grand Line in one piece. She reads weather with almost uncanny accuracy, draws some of the most precise charts on the seas, and keeps the crew's money from disappearing — usually into her own pocket.

She grew up in a village ruled by the Fish-Man pirate Arlong, who killed her adoptive mother and forced her to draw maps for his crew. For years she pretended to be his ally, stealing treasure to buy her village's freedom, and she did all of it alone.

## Fun Facts

- She has no Devil Fruit and fights with the Clima-Tact, a weather staff originally built for her by Usopp.
- She was raised by the ex-Marine Bell-mère alongside her adoptive sister, Nojiko.
- Her shoulder tattoo was designed to cover the Arlong Pirates' sun mark she was branded with.
- She keeps a grove of Bell-mère's tangerine trees growing aboard the Thousand Sunny.
- Her dream is to draw a complete map of the entire world.

## Best Moments

- **The Arlong Park breakdown** — "Help me, Luffy." She's never asked anyone for anything, and Luffy answers by dropping his hat on her head and walking past her into the fight.
- **Nami vs. Kalifa** — a fight she had no business winning, which she takes apart with weather and nerve instead of strength.
- **Claiming Zeus** — turning one of Big Mom's own thundercloud homies into her personal weapon.
- **Mapping the world** — every island the crew visits goes onto her chart, one piece of her dream at a time.

## Final Thoughts

Nami is the crew's tie to reality — the one who worries, counts the money, and plans the route. When it actually matters, though, she fights with everything she has, and she's come through more than almost anyone on the ship.
